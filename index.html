The Process

1. The Idea

Each participating member will come up of an idea they would like to build.

2. Voting Process

Each participating member will cast a vote on a project followed by team formation..

3. Development

Teams will begin developing their project for the maximum duration of 12 days.

4. Showcase

Teams will present their project to the rest of the teams.

FAQ

What is Cod3Hub?

Our goal is to create an online community where developers come together to build and work on projects by participating in hackathon events. Each hackathon event will happen over a span of 12 days where each team will have the oppportunity to come up with an idea and develop it into completion. At the end of the event each team will showcase their final project to the rest of the teams.

Why 12 days?

We understand that this is a remote hackathon and there will be members participating from all over the world residing in different timezones. We didn't want to make the event the typical 24-48hrs and also didn't want to make it too long either. We felt that 12 days is a good balance and would provide sufficient time for teams to be able to plan, design, develop and deploy their projects.

How much experience do I need?

Anyone is able to participate regardless of skill level although having basic experience with HTML, CSS and JavaScript would be nice.

What kind of projects can I build?

You are able to build any type of project as long as it is SFW (Safe For Work).

Is this a good place to practice my programming skills?

You learn by building and making mistakes. We understand that working alone can be boring and frustrating especially when you need a helping hand and we hope that by working on projects in a collaborative environment that it will accelerate your learning while gaining experience all at the same time.

In addition, during our off week we will be posting daily LeetCode problems that helps keeps your programming skills sharp while at the same time prepares you for technical interviews.
